FAQ

How Margin compares

How is Margin different from dbt?

dbt builds data models you author in a warehouse. Margin discovers how metrics are already calculated across your spreadsheets and models, reconciles conflicts, and keeps one governed metric registry.

Do I have to replace dbt or my warehouse?

No. Margin sits on top of the tools you already use and flags when their definitions disagree.

How is this different from Claude Code integrations?

Coding assistants help engineers write code. Margin answers investors' metric questions with governed, source-backed definitions in Slack, Teams, and Linear.

Where do the answers come from?

Every answer is tied to its source and the approved definition in your metric registry, so nothing is invented.
